# Purpose

Active Directory is the identity backbone of many enterprise environments.

The objective is to understand how identities, systems, permissions, and trust relationships are organized.

The focus is not individual users.

The focus is enterprise identity architecture.

---

# Mission Objectives

• Forest structure

• Domains

• Trust relationships

• Users

• Groups

• Administrative delegation

• Authentication architecture
