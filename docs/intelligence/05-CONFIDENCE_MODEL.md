# Purpose

Confidence measures how strongly Trident believes an observation or relationship is supported by available evidence.

Confidence is not truth.

Confidence represents the current level of certainty.

As additional evidence is collected, confidence may increase, decrease, or remain unchanged.

Confidence allows intelligence to evolve without rewriting history.

---

# Definition

Confidence is a measure of evidentiary support.

Confidence belongs to:

• Observations

• Relationships

Confidence does not belong to:

• Entities

Entities either exist or they do not.

Confidence reflects how well we understand them.

---

# Philosophy

Truth is discovered.

Confidence is earned.

Confidence should never be assumed.

Every increase in confidence should be supported by evidence.

---

# Confidence Levels

UNKNOWN

Insufficient information.

Observation recorded.

Evidence limited.

No reliable conclusion.

---

LOW

Initial observation.

Single source.

Requires additional validation.

---

MEDIUM

Multiple supporting observations.

Reasonable confidence.

Independent verification recommended.

---

HIGH

Multiple independent evidence sources.

Consistent observations.

Repeatable.

Highly reliable.

---

VERIFIED

Evidence independently confirms the observation or relationship beyond reasonable operational doubt.

Verification should be rare.

Reserve this level for exceptional certainty.

Confidence may also decrease if contradictory evidence is discovered.

Intelligence should adapt to new information.

---

# Confidence Factors

Confidence may increase through:

Independent observations

Additional evidence

Mission correlation

Repeated validation

Trusted sources

Historical consistency

Confidence may decrease through:

Conflicting evidence

Source unreliability

Expired information

Contradictory observations

Loss of supporting evidence

---

# Confidence Principles

Never inflate confidence.

Never guess.

Unknown is acceptable.

False certainty is dangerous.

Confidence should be explainable.

If confidence cannot be justified,

it should be lowered.

---
