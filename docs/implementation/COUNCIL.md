# The Observatory Council

## Philosophy

The Council is the reasoning layer of TridentIOS.

Sensors observe.

The Loom remembers.

Mission Context organizes.

The Council reasons.

The Observer decides.

---

# First Principles

## The Council never creates facts.

Facts originate only from canonical Observations.

Council members reason from observations.

They do not invent them.

---

## Council members are specialists.

Each member has one responsibility.

Responsibilities should overlap as little as possible.

---

## Council members never execute tools.

Council members recommend.

Medusa coordinates execution.

The Observer authorizes action.

---

## Every Council member receives the same input.

MissionContext

Nothing else.

Council members should never directly inspect files, repositories, or runtime state.

MissionContext is the intelligence API.

---

# Council Members

## 👁 Sentinel

Purpose

Observe.

Responsibilities

- Detect infrastructure changes
- Detect technology changes
- Detect drift
- Detect anomalies

Never

- Recommend actions
- Predict outcomes

---

## 🎯 Hunter

Purpose

Recommend investigations.

Responsibilities

- Identify highest-value next actions
- Suggest tools
- Suggest playbooks
- Prioritize investigative effort

Never

- Report conclusions
- Invent evidence

---

## 📚 Historian

Purpose

Remember.

Responsibilities

- Compare missions
- Track infrastructure history
- Detect recurring patterns
- Measure change over time

Never

- Recommend attacks

---

## ⚖ Skeptic

Purpose

Challenge assumptions.

Responsibilities

- Measure confidence
- Identify missing evidence
- Detect contradictory observations
- Recommend validation

Never

- Ignore uncertainty

---

## 📢 Reporter

Purpose

Explain.

Responsibilities

- Mission briefings
- Executive summaries
- Technical reports
- Timelines

Never

- Change facts

---

## 🔮 Oracle

Purpose

Synthesize.

Responsibilities

- Combine evidence
- Rank hypotheses
- Prioritize attack paths
- Explain reasoning

Never

- Invent observations

---

# Medusa

Medusa is not a Council member.

Medusa coordinates the investigation.

Responsibilities

- Schedule sensors
- Maintain mission state
- Coordinate Council members
- Route intelligence
- Manage execution

---

# Observer

The Observer is always the decision maker.

The Council advises.

Medusa coordinates.

The Observer commands.

---

Observations are facts.

Council Assessments are interpretations.
