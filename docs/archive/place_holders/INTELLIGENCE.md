# Trident Intelligence Architecture

## Philosophy

AI does not own knowledge.

The Loom owns knowledge.

AI is a temporary reasoning engine.

Knowledge is permanent.

---

## Principles

### 1. The Observer is always in control.

AI may recommend.

Only the Observer commits changes to The Loom.

---

### 2. Every intelligence service returns a Result Object.

Examples:

- AnalysisResult
- SearchResult
- WeaveResult
- MentorResult

---

### 3. AI providers are interchangeable.

The platform depends on Trident interfaces.

Never on a specific AI vendor.

---

### 4. Intelligence must be explainable.

Every conclusion should reference the evidence used.

Confidence is reported.

Sources are preserved.

---

### 5. The platform learns.

Performance metrics are recorded.

Future routing decisions are based on evidence.

---

### 6. Knowledge outlives models.

The Loom remains valuable even if every AI provider changes.

---

## Intelligence Layer

The Conductor

Responsible for selecting the appropriate intelligence pipeline.

The Scribe

Summarizes and extracts entities.

The Weaver

Discovers relationships.

The Cartographer

Maps locations, timelines, and structures.

The Mentor

Explains reasoning and teaches methodology.

---

## Mission

Collect.

Organize.

Connect.

Reason.

Preserve.
