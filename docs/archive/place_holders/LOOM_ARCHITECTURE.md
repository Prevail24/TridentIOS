# THE LOOM
## Architecture Doctrine

*"The Loom remembers what others forget."*

---

# Purpose

The Loom is the institutional memory of The Watchers.

It does not exist to store files.

It exists to preserve knowledge.

Every investigation, observation, piece of evidence, relationship, and conclusion becomes part of a permanent intelligence graph that future Observers can build upon.

The Loom is designed so that knowledge never needs to be rediscovered.

---

# Core Philosophy

Trident is not an AI application.

Trident is an Intelligence Operating System.

Artificial Intelligence exists to assist Observers.

The Loom exists to remember.

The Observer remains responsible for judgment.

Evidence remains the source of truth.

---

# The Watchers Doctrine

Observe.

Preserve.

Connect.

Understand.

Share.

These five principles guide every architectural decision within Trident.

---

## Observe

Capture reality exactly as it exists.

Never alter original evidence.

Record facts before conclusions.

---

## Preserve

Nothing important should be forgotten.

Every Observation, Mission, Evidence object, Entity, Timeline, and Report becomes part of The Loom.

Knowledge survives beyond individual investigators.

---

## Connect

Knowledge gains value through relationships.

A single Observation has limited meaning.

Hundreds of connected Observations become intelligence.

Relationships are first-class citizens.

---

## Understand

Artificial Intelligence assists understanding.

It does not replace human reasoning.

AI summarizes.

AI discovers patterns.

AI proposes hypotheses.

Humans make decisions.

---

## Share

Intelligence has value only when it can be communicated.

Reports should always remain traceable back to their supporting evidence.

Transparency is more important than certainty.

---

Each object owns only its own data.

## Everything is Connected.

Objects gain meaning through relationships.

Relationships are never accidental.

Relationships are explicit.

Example:

    contains

    contains
Evidence

    mentions
Entity

    owns

    resolves_to

Relationships form The Knowledge Graph.

---

## The Knowledge Graph

The Knowledge Graph is the structural representation of The Loom.

Objects become Nodes.

Relationships become Edges.

The graph is the source of truth for reasoning.

Artificial Intelligence consumes the graph.

It does not replace it.
